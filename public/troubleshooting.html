Troubleshooting

The URLs on the navbar aren't working

If the URLs aren't working, it probably means that your offline or your internet connection is poor. It also might mean that the URL worked but is taking a long time to redirect. If your browser has a loading bar, you can look at the progress

The website is taking a very long time to load (over 8 seconds)

If it's taking a long time to load, it can mean that your internet connection is poor or it's your first time on the site and the browser hasn't cached the site properly. It can also mean that the website itself is becoming slow. To fix this, you can try refreshing the page to make the browser cache and you can try adding the website as a app. Depending on your browser, there should be a "add to home screen" option that will add the website as an app which will give you faster performance

The game isn't working (stuck on black screen)

If the game is stuck on a black screen, that means that the code isn't compatible with your browser or device. To fix this, update your device or browser. If that still doesn't work, it can mean that your browser is old or faulty. Try using modern browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc. If that doesn't work, try powering your device on and off and checking your internet connection. If none of the above works, it can mean that the game isn't supported in your device
